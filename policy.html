PRIVACY POLICY

Looney Movers ("we", "us", or "our") is a moving company based in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, Canada. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or book our services.

We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. We collect only what we need to provide you with moving services and get back to you with a quote.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you fill out our quote form, call us, or contact us by email, we may collect the following:

  • Your name and contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Your current and destination addresses for the move
  • Details about your move (home size, move date, services needed)
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide in the notes field

We do not collect payment information through this website. Any payment is handled in person or via a secure third-party processor at time of service.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To contact you with a quote for your move
  • To confirm and coordinate your booking
  • To send service-related updates (reminders, confirmations)
  • To improve our services based on customer feedback

We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. We will not add you to email lists or send unsolicited promotional messages.

HOW WE STORE YOUR INFORMATION

Quote requests submitted through our website are received via email to our business inbox. We retain customer information for a reasonable period for service delivery and record-keeping purposes. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

DO WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION?

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited information only in the following circumstances:

  • With our moving crew members to the extent necessary to complete your move
  • If required by law or a lawful government request
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of Looney Movers or others

COOKIES & WEBSITE ANALYTICS

Our website may use basic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. These tools may set cookies to track anonymous usage data such as pages visited and time on site. No personally identifiable information is collected through cookies.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use our website.

THIRD-PARTY LINKS

Our website contains links to third-party platforms such as Google and Facebook. Once you leave our site, their own privacy policies apply. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct inaccurate information, or request that we delete your information. To make any such request, contact us using the details below.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our services are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our website following any updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

TERMS OF SERVICE

These Terms of Service govern your use of the Looney Movers website and the moving services we provide. By booking our services or using our website, you agree to these terms. Please read them carefully.

We aim to be fair and straightforward. These terms exist to protect both you and us. If anything is unclear, just call us — we're real people.

1. BOOKING & QUOTES

All quotes provided by Looney Movers are estimates based on the information you provide. Final pricing is confirmed before work begins. We reserve the right to adjust a quote if the scope of work materially differs from what was described (e.g. significantly more items than disclosed, access issues not mentioned).

  • Quotes are provided free of charge and without obligation
  • A booking is confirmed once we have agreed on a date and time
  • We do not require a deposit for most bookings
  • Same-day and short-notice moves are subject to availability

2. PAYMENT

Payment is due upon completion of the move unless otherwise agreed in writing. We accept cash, e-Transfer, and credit card. Applicable GST will be added to all invoices.

  • Hourly moves are billed in 30-minute increments after the minimum
  • Flat-rate moves are billed at the agreed amount regardless of time
  • Additional services added on the day of the move will be billed at our standard rates

3. CANCELLATIONS & RESCHEDULING

We understand plans change. We ask for as much notice as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule.

  • Cancellations with 48+ hours notice: no charge
  • Cancellations with 24–48 hours notice: may be subject to a cancellation fee
  • Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice or no-shows: a cancellation fee may apply
  • Looney Movers reserves the right to reschedule in cases of severe weather, equipment failure, or other circumstances beyond our control

4. LIABILITY & DAMAGE

Looney Movers takes every precaution to protect your belongings. We are fully insured. However, the following conditions apply:

  • We are liable for damage caused by our crew's negligence during the move
  • We are not liable for damage to items that were pre-existing, improperly packed by the customer, or inherently fragile without disclosure
  • High-value or irreplaceable items should be disclosed before the move
  • Damage claims must be reported within 48 hours of move completion
  • Our liability is limited to the replacement or repair value of the damaged item, not sentimental value

Please disclose any specialty, fragile, or high-value items before your move so we can take appropriate precautions. Items like pianos, antiques, artwork, and electronics require special handling.

5. CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES

To ensure a smooth move, we ask that you:

  • Provide accurate information about the scope of the move when booking
  • Ensure clear access to both pickup and destination locations
  • Disclose any stairs, elevators, long carries, or tight spaces in advance
  • Have all items ready to be moved at the agreed start time
  • Ensure any items containing hazardous materials are not included in the move
  • Be present or have an authorized representative present during the move

6. PROHIBITED ITEMS

We cannot transport the following items:

  • Hazardous materials, flammable liquids, explosives, or chemicals
  • Illegal substances or contraband
  • Firearms or ammunition (unless properly declared and legal)
  • Perishable food items on long-distance moves
  • Live animals or plants

7. WEBSITE USE

By using the Looney Movers website, you agree not to misuse the site, submit false information through our quote form, or attempt to interfere with the site's functionality. All content on this website is the property of Looney Movers and may not be reproduced without permission.

8. GOVERNING LAW

These Terms of Service are governed by the laws of the Province of Saskatchewan and the applicable federal laws of Canada. Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Saskatchewan.

9. CHANGES TO THESE TERMS

We may update these Terms from time to time. Continued use of our website or services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
